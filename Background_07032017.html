Luke Skywalker
A young boy who thinks he is an orphan and lives with his aunt and uncle. He wants to become a jedi and follows R2D2 and C3PO on a mission to save Princess Leia, his twin sister, where he meets Yoda who teaches him the ways of a jedi.
Leia Organa
The leader of the Rebel Alliance and the Resistance who has been captured by Darth Vader and seeks assistance from Luke and Han Solo to save her. She later gets married to Han Solo.
Darth Vader
An ex-jedi whose real name is Anakin Sywalker. He was taught the ways of a jedi by Obi Wan-Kenobi, but was influenced by the dark side adn became a sith lord. He is Luke and Leia's father and has joined the dark side to destroy the remaining jedis left in the galaxy.
Yoda
A jedi master who tains Luke and is a part of the Rebellion, which fights against the dark side. He goes into exile after the Republic falls.
Han Solo
A young captain of the Millenium Falcon who joins the Rebellion and gets married to Leia. He helps Luke on Luke's journey to save his twin.
C-3PO
A protocol droid that Luke bargains for who helped him on his mission to save Leia and defeat Darth Vader.
